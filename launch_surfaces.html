Reading notes

  1. wiom.tv is a portal-distributed product: 86.6% of launches arrive from the i2e1 wifi login/landing flow. Every other surface is a rounding error next to it — which makes the portal handoff experience the highest-leverage acquisition surface.
  2. Installed-PWA users behave like a different species: 50% activation, median 16 channel changes and 3.4 watch-minutes per user. This is partly selection (engaged users install), but it means the install banner (see the Add to Home Screen tab) is the product's best lever for converting portal drive-by traffic into a durable audience.
  3. “Direct / saved link” is a mixed bag: empty referrer includes typed URLs, bookmarks, and referrer-stripping apps like WhatsApp — plus 191 pre-Jun-27 sessions that could be undetected standalone launches (launch-mode detection began Jun 27).
  4. Payment success page converts respectably (19% activation, median 5 channel changes) but watch time stays low — users sample, then leave.
  5. Search is negligible (13 sessions, 15 days) — there is no meaningful organic discovery today; the 55.6% activation is 5 of 9 users, not a signal.
  6. Surface overlap is low: 4.2% of users appear under more than one surface, so per-surface user metrics are only mildly contaminated.
