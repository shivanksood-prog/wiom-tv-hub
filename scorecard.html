Users Dashboard A2HS Deep-Dive

Total Watch Hours per Day · New vs Repeating share

Total watch = DAU × sessions/user × watch-min/session. DAU and sessions/user live on the Users Dashboard; this card and the next complete the decomposition. Repeating users carry roughly half the hours with a quarter of the users.

Watch Minutes per Session

Third factor of the decomposition. Means are whale-sensitive — a single left-on device moves the repeating line by minutes.

Watch per Active User-Day · P75 & P90 minutes

Tune-dwell summed per user per IST day; only users with ≥1 tune that day count. The Users Dashboard shows median & P90 — P75 is added here.

Retention by First-Seen Cohort · D1 and D15

D1 = returned the very next IST day. D15 = returned on any IST day ≥15 after first seen (unbounded) — plotted only for cohorts old enough to have a full 15-day window. New-user metrics by definition, so no new/repeat cut.

Add to Home Screen · % of New Users Installing in Their First Session

"Install" = answered the native prompt (pwa_install); accept vs decline isn't persisted server-side, and iOS installs are invisible — treat as an upper-bound leading indicator. New-user metric by definition.

Playback Errors per 100 Tunes

playback_error events over tune attempts. E4 (Jul 13, PR#8) removed 5 dead channels — the drop after it is channel cleanup, not user behaviour.
